When it comes to suggestions for your activity tracker page, you might consider the following functionalities:

    Filtering Options: Allow filtering by user, activity type, or date range.
    Real-time Update: Use AJAX to periodically check for new activities and update the page without requiring a full refresh.
    Detail Expansion: Click on an activity log entry to see more details or context.
    User Linking: Make usernames clickable to go to the user's profile or activity history.
